Totchos

Description

Nachos, with tater tots instead of corn chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 (32 ounce) package frozen tots
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix, or equivalent spices
  • 2 cups shredded cheese, any kind
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (or plain yogurt)
  • lime juice
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup jalapeno pepper slices
  • 1 sliced avocado
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spread tots out onto a baking sheet.
  2. Bake tots in the preheated oven until golden brown and crispy (~22min, or follow directions on bag).
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add water and taco seasoning to beef; bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until liquid reduces, about 5 minutes.
  4. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler. Arrange potato nuggets on an oven-proof platter and cover evenly with ground beef; spread cheese over the top.
  5. Cook potato nuggets under the broiler until cheese melts, 1 to 3 minutes. Whisk sour cream and lime juice together in a bowl. Top with tomatoes, jalapenos, avocado, sour cream mixture, onions, and cilantro, respectively.
